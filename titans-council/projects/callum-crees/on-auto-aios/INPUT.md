# Project Brief: Callum Crees — On Auto / AIOS

## What Is This?

**On Auto** is an AI sales infrastructure service for growing businesses. Callum Crees is the founder. He installs AI-powered sales systems into businesses — speed-to-lead agents, follow-up agents, reactivation agents, and a full AI Operating System (AIOS) for businesses that want to go further.

He is NOT a software company. He is NOT a consultancy. He builds and runs the systems for you.

## The Core Offer: On Auto

**On Auto** installs AI-powered sales infrastructure into your business so your pipeline runs on autopilot, your leads are worked properly, and your team closes more with less effort.

### What Gets Installed

1. **Speed-to-Lead Agent** — Responds to every inbound lead within 60 seconds, 24/7. Qualifies, books, and hands off to the sales team. No more leads going cold overnight.

2. **Follow-Up Agent** — Automated multi-touch sequences triggered by CRM actions. Every prospect gets followed up. Nothing falls through the cracks.

3. **Reactivation Agent** — Re-engages cold and dead leads with personalised outreach. Recovers money already spent on lead acquisition.

4. **AI Operating System (AIOS)** — For businesses ready to go further: a full intelligence layer wrapped around operations. The AI knows the business, watches the numbers, and surfaces what matters daily.

### How It Works

1. **Audit** — Map the current sales process and identify where revenue is leaking
2. **Build** — Install and configure the agents specific to the setup and CRM
3. **Run** — Manage and optimise the systems on a monthly retainer

### Pricing

- **On Auto (core agents):** £500 deposit → £2,500 on completion → £500/month ongoing
- **AIOS (full intelligence layer):** Custom pricing based on the business owner and scope
- Only delivered for two clients so far, both through Callum's existing network

## The Problem He Solves

Most businesses don't lose deals because of bad salespeople. They lose them because of bad systems.

- A lead comes in at 9pm — no one responds until morning
- A prospect books a call — no one follows up when they no-show
- A customer goes cold six months ago — no one's touched them since

These aren't people problems. They're process problems. And they're costing revenue every single week.

## Who Is It For?

Growing businesses (likely SMBs, £500K-£5M revenue range based on pricing) who:
- Have leads coming in but aren't working them fast enough
- Have a sales team but no systems to support them
- Have a dead leads database they've never reactivated
- Are spending on ads/marketing but leaking revenue in the sales process

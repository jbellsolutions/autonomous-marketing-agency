## Quick Reference: The Key Decisions

Here's the council's unanimous recommendation in plain English:

**What to call yourself:** Revenue Infrastructure Company (not AI agency)

**Your mechanism name:** The Revenue Leak Protocol

**Your tagline:** "Revenue infrastructure, installed and running."

**Your anti-category line:** "I don't sell AI. I stop revenue leaks."

**Your three leaks:**
1. The Speed Gap — leads arrive after hours, nobody responds until morning
2. The Follow-Up Failure — prospects no-show and never hear from you again
3. The Dead Database — hundreds of leads sitting in the CRM, untouched

**Your three phases:**
1. Audit — find the leaks, put a pound figure on them
2. Seal — install the agents at each leak point
3. Lock — manage and optimise permanently

**Your front-end entry point:** Revenue Leak Calculator (5-7 questions, gives them an estimated leak figure)

**Your conversion event:** Revenue Leak Workshop (45-60 min, teaches the three leaks, reveals the protocol)

**Your close mechanism:** Revenue Leak Audit (free, personalised, shows their specific number)

**Your offer:** GBP 500 audit fee (credited) + GBP 2,500 install + GBP 500/month managed operations

**Your guarantee:** If we can't find leaks worth 10x the setup cost, we say so and don't take your money.

**Your channel (for the next 90 days):** LinkedIn. One platform. One message. 3-5 posts a week.

**What to do about AIOS:** Park it. No public-facing material for 6 months. It's an upsell for existing clients only.

## What to Do This Week

1. **Reposition everything.** Update your LinkedIn headline, bio, and featured section. Update your website. Kill every instance of "AI agency." Replace with revenue infrastructure language.

2. **Get numbers from your two existing clients.** Response time before/after. Leads the system responded to. Reactivation results. Any revenue you can attribute. Even rough numbers are powerful.

3. **Start tracking Revenue Recovered.** Create a running counter of total revenue recovered across all clients. This goes in your email signature, your website, your LinkedIn posts, and every proposal.

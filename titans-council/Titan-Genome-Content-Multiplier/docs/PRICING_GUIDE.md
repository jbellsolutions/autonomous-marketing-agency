## Revenue Model

### Tier 1 (Digital Product)
- **Revenue per sale:** $47-97

- **Scale:** Unlimited -- no fulfillment time per sale
- **Growth lever:** Automated sales funnel, affiliates, content marketing

### Tier 2 (Hybrid)
- **Revenue per client:** $297-497
- **Your time:** 2-3 hours per client
- **Effective hourly rate:** $100-250/hr

- **Revenue per client:** $997-2,497
- **Your time:** 5-10 hours per client (pipeline + QA)

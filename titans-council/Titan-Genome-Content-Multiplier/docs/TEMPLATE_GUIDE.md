---

#### Template 1 of 32: Content Map

**File:** `templates/PROJECT_1_Content_Map.md`

**What It Produces:** A master blueprint that extracts every topic from your video with timestamps, organizes them into a hierarchy, maps difficulty levels, identifies key quotes and story moments, and creates a matrix showing which of the 13 downstream projects each topic feeds. Includes 6 appendices (glossary, tools, demos, audience segments, CTA opportunities, content gaps).

**Target Audience:** Internal use (you and your team). Not published.

**Customization Points:**
- No brand customization needed -- this is analytical, not promotional
- Adjust the number of expected topics based on video length (roughly 10-15 topics per 30 minutes)
- Add or remove appendices based on what is relevant to your content

```
moments, and suggest video cut points.

My two audiences are: {{AUDIENCE_A}} and {{AUDIENCE_B}}
My main offer is: {{YOUR_OFFER}}

Save to ./outputs/Content_Map.md
```

**Time to Produce:** 15-30 minutes (Creator agent)

**Dependencies:** Requires only the transcript. No other templates needed. This MUST be completed before all other projects.

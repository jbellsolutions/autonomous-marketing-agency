## SEQUENCE STRATEGY OVERVIEW

**Email 0 (Day 0):** Welcome + Deliver Promise -- immediate value, set expectations
**Email 1 (Day 1):** Quick Win -- teach something actionable in under 5 minutes
**Email 2 (Day 2):** Deeper Dive -- expand on core concept, build credibility
**Email 3 (Day 3):** Story/Case Study -- social proof through narrative
**Email 4 (Day 4):** Common Mistake -- "stop doing this" pattern interrupt
**Email 5 (Day 5):** Framework/System -- introduce the {{BRAND_NAME}} method
**Email 6 (Day 6):** Objection Handling -- address the #1 reason people hesitate
**Email 7 (Day 7):** Vision Casting -- paint the "after" picture
**Email 8 (Day 8):** Final CTA -- urgency + summary + clear next step
